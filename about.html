We built Batora AI because small businesses deserve better

Our Story
Batora AI
Automation Experts

Batora AI was built because small businesses were losing thousands of hours to work that should never have been manual.

We spent years in the trenches of tech — watching talented, hardworking business owners spend hours every day on tasks a machine could handle in seconds. Following up with leads by hand. Sending the same reminder texts one by one. Copying data between tools that never talked to each other.

The technology to fix all of it already existed — Zapier, Make, AI APIs, CRMs — but connecting it all requires real technical depth that most small business owners simply don't have time to develop. That's the gap Batora AI was built to close.

We work directly with every client. You'll always deal with the people who actually build your automations — not a sales team that hands you off to someone else. That direct relationship is what makes Batora AI different.
